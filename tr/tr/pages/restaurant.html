Restoran & Bars Experience

A gourmet experience flavors that accompany you every moment of the day...

At İnzi, the elegant restaurant of Palmalife Bodrum Resort & Spa, indulge in the freshest Aegean flavors and refined tastes from international cuisine served 24/7 with a panoramic sea view.

Whether you start your day with a rich breakfast at the break of dawn or treat yourself to a romantic dinner at sunset, İnzi is here to elevate every moment.

If you wish, these unique flavors can also be delivered to the comfort of your room with the privilege of 24-hour room service.

Wherever you are, quality and taste are always together on your holiday.

Palmalife Hotel

Bars Experience

Feel the soul of Bodrum in every sip...

At Palmalife Bodrum Resort & Spa, our drink experience is more than just refreshment, it's a lifestyle. From signature cocktails to fresh fruit blends and premium local & international selections, enjoy indulgence across three distinct bar concepts.

  • Life Bar

    Located at the heart of the hotel, Life Bar offers the perfect setting to relax, socialize, and savor delightful moments throughout the day.

  • Pool Bar

    Reenergize under the sun with refreshing drinks and light bites by the poolside. The Pool Bar is your companion for a cool, carefree day.

  • Beach Bar

    With your feet in the sand and a chilled cocktail in hand, the Beach Bar brings the ultimate beachside bliss right by the waves.
