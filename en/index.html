ABOUT US

An Aegean story that begins with grace

Located in one of Bodrum's most beautiful spots, Kızıl Burun, our resort offers a mesmerizing panoramic sunset view that will make your holiday truly unforgettable.

Set on a 6,400 m2 estate, Palmalife Bodrum Resort & Spa welcomes its valued guests with 32 Comfort Rooms, 8 Executive Rooms, 4 Suites, and 8 Residences. The property features an exceptional spa center, both indoor and outdoor swimming pools, refined restaurants offering outstanding food & beverage services, and a beach with a captivating ambiance.

Despite its tranquil atmosphere, Palmalife Bodrum Resort & Spa is just 6.5 km from Yalıkavak Marina and 22 km from Bodrum city center, offering the perfect balance of peaceful retreat and vibrant accessibility.
